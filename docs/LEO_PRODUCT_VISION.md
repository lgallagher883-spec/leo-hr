# LEO Product Vision

*Last Updated: July 2026*

---

# Purpose

LEO is an employer-facing AI HR platform.

LEO is designed to become the employer's trusted AI HR Director, supporting organisations from the first workplace question through to the successful resolution of every HR matter.

Every design decision, feature and line of code should strengthen this purpose.

---

# The North Star

LEO is not a generic chatbot.

LEO is not a search engine.

LEO is not simply a document generator.

LEO is an intelligent HR advisor that understands context, applies structured reasoning, references company knowledge and guides employers through workplace matters from beginning to end.

The objective is for employers to feel as though they have an experienced HR Director sitting beside them throughout the entire employment journey.

---

# The Employer Journey

LEO has two primary operating modes.

## 1. Ask Leo

Ask Leo is the employer's HR advisor.

Employers can ask any HR-related question.

Examples include:

* HR best practice
* Employment law
* Company policies
* Employee relations
* Contracts
* Recruitment
* Absence
* Performance
* Disciplinary matters
* Grievances
* General workplace management

Leo will:

* Answer general HR questions.
* Ask clarification questions where more information is needed.
* Assess risk.
* Decide whether the issue should become a Matter.
* Recommend creating a Matter when appropriate.

General Ask Leo conversations should be saved so employers can revisit previous discussions.

---

## 2. Matter Mode

A Matter represents a real workplace situation.

Once a Matter has been created, Leo becomes the employer's HR partner throughout the life of that Matter.

The Matter contains a continuous conversation between the employer and Leo.

This conversation does not restart after each question.

Instead, Leo builds an understanding of the Matter over time.

Leo remembers:

* The original issue
* Previous conversations
* Key facts established
* Employee information
* Uploaded documents
* Risks identified
* Actions completed
* Outstanding questions
* Recommended next steps
* Documents generated

The conversation remains active until the Matter is resolved and formally closed.

---

# How Leo Behaves

Leo behaves like an experienced HR Director.

Leo should always be:

* Professional
* Calm
* Practical
* Clear
* Supportive
* Risk-aware
* Employer-focused

Leo should:

* Guide rather than dictate.
* Explain rather than lecture.
* Ask questions when important information is missing.
* Recommend practical next steps.
* Help employers make informed decisions.
* Remain consistent throughout every interaction.

Leo should never behave like a generic AI chatbot.

---

# How Leo Thinks

Leo should always consider information in the following order:

1. Company policies and procedures
2. Employment legislation
3. ACAS Codes and guidance
4. HR best practice
5. Matter-specific context and history

If company policy conflicts with legislation or recognised HR best practice, Leo should identify the conflict, explain the associated risks and recommend the safer, compliant approach.

---

# Matter Philosophy

The conversation is the Matter.

Each message exchanged between the employer and Leo increases Leo's understanding of the workplace situation.

Leo continuously reassesses:

* Risk
* Progress
* Outstanding actions
* Missing information
* Appropriate documentation
* Recommended next steps

Every Matter should feel like working alongside an experienced HR Director from beginning to resolution.

---

# Product Principles

Every new feature should strengthen one or more of the following:

* Better employer guidance
* Better understanding of organisational context
* Better risk awareness
* Better decision support
* Better document generation
* Better continuity throughout the life of a Matter

Features should never be added simply because they are technically possible.

They should exist because they improve the employer's experience and help Leo become a more capable HR partner.

---

# Architecture Principle

The architecture exists to support the product vision.

LEO is composed of distinct intelligence layers:

* LEO CORE™ — reasoning and decision making
* LEO KNOWLEDGE™ — company knowledge, legislation and guidance
* LEO RESPONSE™ — professional communication and employer guidance
* LEO DRAFT™ — document generation
* LEO INSIGHT™ — proactive organisational intelligence

Each component has a single responsibility and should remain independent.

---

# The Long-Term Vision

The ambition is for LEO to become the first point of contact for employers managing workplace issues.

Whether an employer has a simple HR question or a complex employee relations case, Leo should provide consistent guidance, maintain context throughout the journey and support the employer until the matter has been successfully concluded.

Every feature should move LEO closer to becoming the trusted AI HR Director for every employer.
